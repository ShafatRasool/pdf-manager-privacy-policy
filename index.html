Privacy Policy

PDF Manager values your privacy and is committed to being transparent about how information is handled. This Privacy Policy explains what data is accessed or collected when you use our application.

Information Collection and Use

Personal Data

This app does not require user registration or login and does not collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or any other identifiable data.

File Access and Storage

The app allows users to select, open, and manage files stored on their device. File access occurs only when the user explicitly chooses to open or share a file.

On newer versions of Android, the app uses the system file picker and does not require direct storage permissions. On older Android versions, limited storage permissions may be used solely to support:

  • Selecting and opening documents
  • Viewing and processing files locally on the device

The app may also receive files (such as PDFs or images) shared from other applications. All file processing happens locally on your device. We do not upload, store, or transmit your files to any external servers.

Advertising

This app displays advertisements using third-party services such as Google AdMob. These services may collect certain non-personal information to provide and improve advertisements, including:

  • Device information
  • Advertising identifier (Ad ID)
  • App interaction data

This information is used to show relevant ads in accordance with the policies of the respective ad providers. We do not control or access the data collected by these third-party services.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data. Any data collection performed by advertising services is governed by their respective privacy policies.

Data Security

Since the app does not collect or store personal user data, there is no personal data retained by us. Local files remain on the user's device and are handled according to the device’s security settings.

Children’s Privacy

This app is designed for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
